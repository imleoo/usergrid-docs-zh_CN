Realtime Analytics¶

Most applications today need to provide analytics capabilities for business intelligence, revenue generation, product improvement, and end-user functionality. For example, for most apps, you want to be able to know how often people are launching your application, whether certain features are popular, and other statistics of user behavior for sponsorship or advertising purposes. Usergrid provides a very flexible mechanism for keeping track of any sorts of metrics.
